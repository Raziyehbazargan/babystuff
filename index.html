Further Safety Information

The United States Consumer Product Safety Commission(CPSC) regulates the sale and manufacture of different consumer products from cribs to all-terrain vehicles. The CPSC does this by banning dangerous products, establishing safety requirements, issuing recalls of products already on the market, and researching potential hazards associated with consumer products.
